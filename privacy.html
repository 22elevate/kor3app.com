Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Overview

KOR3 is developed by 22 Elevate, LLC. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use the KOR3 app.

Information We Collect

KOR3 stores your data locally on your device only. We collect: your first name (for personalization), your daily task entries, your completion history, and your streak count. None of this data is transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide the core features of KOR3 — displaying your tasks, tracking your streak, and personalizing your experience with your name. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

Subscription & Payments

KOR3 offers a 7-day free trial followed by a paid subscription ($2.99/month or $19.99/year). All payments are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your payment information. Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time through your device's subscription settings.

Data Storage

All personal data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed. We do not maintain any backups of your personal data on external servers.

Third-Party Services

KOR3 uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymized purchase data in accordance with their privacy policy. KOR3 does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

KOR3 is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is described in this policy.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app. You can also reset your name and streak from the Settings screen within the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at life@kor3app.com
